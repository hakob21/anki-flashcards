Move to next word occurrence in one keystroke on Vim

When your cursor is under a word, you can hit * (asterisk key) to jump to this next word occurrence. It is a shortcut for searching this word.

You can then press N and n to navigate back and forth the results.

A simple Vim movement that took me 2 years to hear about!

Did you know it already?
